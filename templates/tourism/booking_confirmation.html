What Happens Next?

Step 1: Confirmation Email

You'll receive a detailed confirmation email within 15 minutes. Check your inbox (and spam folder) for booking details.

Step 2: Tour Guide Contact

Your assigned tour guide will contact you 24 hours before the tour via WhatsApp/SMS with meeting point details.

Step 3: Day of the Tour

Arrive 15 minutes early at the meeting point with your booking ID and valid ID/passport.

Step 4: Enjoy Your Tour!

Experience the best of Tanzania with our expert guides. Don't forget to share your photos with #AFCON2027Tanzania

Important Information

  • Free cancellation available up to 24 hours before the tour
  • Bring your booking confirmation (digital or printed)
  • Wear comfortable shoes and bring water for outdoor tours
  • For AFCON match days, allow extra travel time
